Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

Got Questions?

We're here to help!

How long does delivery take?

We offer same-day delivery within Nairobi for orders placed before 2 PM. For surrounding counties, delivery typically takes 24-48 hours. Emergency orders can be arranged with additional charges.

Do you sell to hotels or bulk buyers?

Yes! We have special pricing for bulk orders (10+ birds). We supply several hotels and restaurants in Nairobi. Contact us for a custom quote and regular supply arrangements.

Can I visit the farm?

Absolutely! Farm visits are welcome between 8 AM - 5 PM daily. Please call ahead to arrange your visit. You can see our chickens, learn about our farming practices, and pick up your order directly.

Are your chickens vaccinated?

Yes, all our chickens receive essential vaccinations as per veterinary guidelines. We maintain proper health records and work with certified veterinarians to ensure healthy birds.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-PESA (most popular), cash on delivery, and bank transfers. For bulk orders, we can arrange payment terms. Payment is typically required before delivery or upon delivery for cash customers.

How do you ensure freshness?

We slaughter chickens fresh upon order - never from frozen stock. For live delivery, chickens are transported in proper ventilated cages. Cleaned chickens are hygienically packaged and delivered within hours of processing.
